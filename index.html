A Gateway into Gaming

A catalog of game consoles & their (averaged) prices!

With the rising interest in retro gaming, the cost to get into such products increases and fluctuates every day. With so many potential avenues for getting into this hobby, people will naturally want to figure out what options will be the most cost-efficient.

Things to Note

  • Current iteration will focus on gaming devices from the 2000s-2010s.
  • For home consoles, intended methods of display (such as CRT TVs & component/composite cables) will not be factored into the prices of the individual products.
  • Information is sourced from www.pricecharting.com, thus aspects such as product condition will not be considered.
